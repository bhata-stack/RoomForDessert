Tofu Bao 10/05/2024

Yum Dum Food Truck

Lunch

I got two tofu baos from the Yum Dum food truck at Apple Fest in Lincoln Square. Now, you may be wondering where the apples are in tofu baos. The tofu baos are topped with apple kimchi, which is really unique and goes nicely with the rest of the ingredients. The spicy and sweet sauce paired well with the crispy tofu, and the kimchi added just the bit extra it needed in texture and flavor. Overall, I enjoyed it.

It's a real interesting experience eating while standing in the sun and hearing the train go by every now and then, overpowering the sound of the crowd and the live music performance.
